Vetro

A Fiber Network Planning and Management Tool

Vetro makes it easier to target your fiber network investment and manage it for the long term.

  • Track demand by neighborhood and drive interest to your project.
  • Create project scenarios and estimate project costs.
  • View your fiber network assets including cables, poles, material, and equipment.

"As a small fiber network provider we will use Vetro to help identify and manage demand as well as for project design and cost estimation" - Fiber Network Engineer

Vetro consists of three modules: asset inventory mapping and display; demand generation; and project design and planning.

  1. Asset inventory mapping and display - planners and engineers will be able view assets, lit buildings, data centers, potential customers, rights-of-way, conduits, and other features through a simple mapping interface;
  2. Demand generation and tracking - tools that allow sales people and planners to estimate and track the demand for fiber in particular regions; and
  3. Project Design and Planning - Vetro will provide simple features that allow fiber planners and engineers to design and plan new projects. Design and planning includes estimating the type and quantity of equipment that is required, poles and/or conduits that can be used to place the fiber, where splice points will occur, and what points of presence (POPs) will be used.

FAQ

What does Vetro mean?
Vetro is the Italian word for glass, the material that makes up a fiber strand.
Do I need to license any additonal software to run Vetro?
No, Vetro is an entirely secure, cloud-based application that is built using opne source tools and does not require any 3rd party licensing.
